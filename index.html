Research Interests

My main research interests are in applying both linguistic and computational approaches to investigate the properties (such as meaning) of natural language in use through corpora. Topics of interests include discourse and sentiment analysis, natural language processing/understanding, phonetics, syntax-semantics interface, as well as language and speech. These interests have driven me toward the following directions: 

  • Discourse and Sentiment Analysis: How can we effectively analyze discourse in natural languages with various parsing frameworks? 
  • Natural Language Processing/Understanding: How effectively do the models handle human languages? What machine models inform linguists about human languages?
